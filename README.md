## ✨ Features
* 🌸 **8 Custom Kawaii Levels:** Play levels like *Sakura Dream*, *Moon Bunny*, and *Rainbow Road*
* 🎨 **Avatar Customizer:** Pick from a pastel color palette and 6 cute shapes (Star, Heart, Diamond, Cat, Flower, or Square)
* 🎶 **Procedural Chiptune Audio:** Generated in real-time
* ✏️ **Built-in Level Editor:** Make your own cute obstacle courses!
* 📱 **Works Anywhere:** Fully playable in browser on desktop, iPad, or mobile!

## 🎮 How to Play
Just click here to play instantly: **[Play Kawaii Dash!](https://alisavasiutenko.github.io/kawaii-dash/)**

**Controls:**
* **Jump / Fly:** Default tap, click, or `Spacebar`
* **Cube:** Tap to jump
* **Ship:** Hold to fly up, release to fall
* **Ball:** Tap to reverse gravity
* **Wave:** Hold to fly diagonally up, release to fly diagonally down
